Introduction to Voktis Group

Voktis Group is a trusted IT support and consultancy provider serving businesses across the UK. Our vision is to empower organisations with reliable, innovative, and secure technology solutions that drive growth and efficiency. From small businesses to established enterprises, we provide tailored IT support that helps companies stay resilient and future-ready.

We believe that strong IT foundations create confident, forward-looking businesses. Our dedication to excellence in IT support stems from our passion to see clients thrive in an ever-evolving digital world. With your trust and partnership, Voktis Group enables businesses to operate seamlessly, embrace innovation, and achieve their full potential.

Our Work Together

For years, Voktis Group has been committed to supporting students by bridging the gap between education and the IT industry. Through initiatives such as the T Level Digital Support Services competition at The Manchester College, students gained hands-on experience in IT infrastructure, networking, and problem-solving. These success stories highlighted the transformative impact that real-world learning, mentorship, and career guidance can have on young people preparing for the future. Building on this legacy, Voktis Group has now developed Brightemy a more comprehensive platform designed to extend these opportunities even further. Brightemy combines structured digital learning with practical training, ensuring that students not only gain technical skills but also the confidence, adaptability, and industry insights needed to thrive in today’s competitive world. What began as workshops, competitions, and mentorship has now evolved into a fully-fledged educational initiative. Brightemy represents Voktis Group’s vision of creating sustainable pathways for lifelong learning, where technology and education work hand in hand to empower the next generation.
